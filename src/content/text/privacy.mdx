## Collecting and Using Your Personal Data
On Our contact page, users if they choose to contact Me; You will have to provide data to be sent to My E-mail Service Provider (Resend). This data is not facilitated on any databases, snapshots, or buckets on the Domain. The data that You will provide is Your:

- Valid Email Address
- Name or Alias
- Message to be sent to Me

All information above is directly sent to the E-mail Service Provider (Resend). No data is scanned or kept, only forwarded to Resend for processing and sending You a confirmation email that Your message has been received. Responses will be responded to by Me via the official Domain handle of: nathan@ncp.dev. All API keys are excluded from the GitHub repository, and not available for the public, if they decide to fork <Link text="ncp.dev" href="https://www.ncp.dev/" />.

### Usage Data
Upon loading any Domain, <Link text="ncp.dev" href="https://www.ncp.dev/" /> uses and sends web analytics to Service Providers (Vercel and MyFonts), to track visited pages (just impressions for MyFonts), errors, and other key events indicated. All data sent and tracked by the Service Provider is anonymous.

### Cookies
No Cookies are filed under any Domain when You surf the Website. This includes any Service Provider registered under the Domain, unless You navigate outside of the Domain. All YouTube embeds are also registered with __no-cookie__ parameters, even though they show up in Cookies.

### Session Data
There are several attributes tracked via session data. I only track two keys, being __direction__ (View Transitions) and __referrer__ (500 Series Errors). YouTube also has session data logs via its embeds, but are completely harmless.
